ViviTorr

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Overview

ViviTorr ("the Bot") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

TL;DR: We collect minimal data, don't store personal information permanently, and never sell your data to anyone.

2. Information We Collect

When you use ViviTorr, we may temporarily process the following information:

  • Discord User ID: Used to manage your active search session
  • Search Queries: The movie/TV show names you search for
  • Command Usage: Which commands you interact with

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect or store:

  • Your Discord username, email, or personal details
  • Your IP address
  • Message content outside of bot commands
  • Which magnet links you click or download
  • Any information from your Discord server

4. How We Use Information

The limited information we process is used solely for:

  • Providing search results and torrent information
  • Managing your active search session (temporary, in-memory only)
  • Caching API responses to improve performance (Redis cache)

5. Data Storage & Retention

Session Data: Your search session is stored temporarily in memory and is automatically deleted when:

  • You complete your search
  • The session expires (typically within 15-30 minutes)
  • The bot restarts

Cache Data: API responses are cached temporarily in Redis to improve performance:

  • TMDB metadata: Up to 1 hour
  • Torrent results: Up to 2 hours

No personal data is included in cached information.

6. Third-Party Services

ViviTorr interacts with third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Discord: Privacy Policy
  • TMDB: Privacy Policy
  • Torrent Indexers: Various third-party services (Torrentio, Comet, etc.)

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect processed data:

  • No persistent storage of personal information
  • Secure connections to third-party APIs
  • Regular security updates

8. Children's Privacy

ViviTorr is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has used our Bot, please contact us.

9. Your Rights

Since we don't store personal data permanently, there is no data to request, modify, or delete. Your session data is automatically removed as described above.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with a new "Last updated" date. Continued use of the Bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Open Source

ViviTorr is open-source software. You can review exactly what data the Bot processes by examining our source code on GitHub.

12. Contact

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please reach out through our mail contact@rishab.cv
